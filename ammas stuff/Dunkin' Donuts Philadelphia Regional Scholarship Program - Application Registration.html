Registration

Dunkin' Donuts and Scholarship Management Services recognize and respect your desire for privacy. This application requires the submission of personal information to Scholarship Management Services directly. Information provided by the applicant is considered confidential and is handled only by Scholarship Management Services. Only authorized persons with a need and right to know will be permitted access to this information. Privacy Policy.

Consent

Applicants must read and agree to the consent statement below.

"I understand that my online application contains confidential personal data. I hereby authorize Scholarship Management Services to review the information in my application for the purpose of determining my eligibility for the Dunkin' Donuts Philadelphia Regional Scholarship Program. I also understand and agree that Scholarship Management Services may transfer, store and process my application data in any of its offices only in conjunction with the selection process for a Dunkin' Donuts Philadelphia Regional Scholarship. I verify that I have read, understand, and agree to the guidelines for this program.

By registering for an application account, I consent to the statement above and to the guidelines for the Dunkin' Donuts Philadelphia Regional Scholarship Program.”

To register as an applicant, the student must provide a unique and valid email address. Correspondence throughout the application process will be via email. Students failing to use a valid email address will not be able to access an application account. Students who do not regularly check their email for correspondence may not receive consideration.
